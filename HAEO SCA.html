About Us

HAEO Sparkle Clean Services is a professional home cleaning service that offers a range of cleaning solutions tailored to meet the needs of busy individuals and families in Buea, Cameroon. Our mission is to provide reliable, high-quality cleaning services that ensure a clean and healthy living environment for our clients.


With years of experience and a dedicated team of cleaning professionals, we take pride in delivering exceptional results that exceed our clients' expectations. We use eco-friendly products and modern cleaning techniques to ensure your space is not just clean, but also safe for your family.
